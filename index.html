How our quiz works
  • Every answer you give updates your real-time skin profile.

How we build your routine
  • We analyze your answers to pinpoint your skin’s hydration, oil balance, barrier strength, exfoliation needs, sensitivities, and lifestyle.
  • We select ingredients by function (brighten, soothe, exfoliate), molecular weight (penetration), solubility & pH (stability), and interaction safety (no conflicts).
  • Your routine is layered like a cosmetic chemist: cleanser, toner/essence (if needed), serum (for your top goal), moisturizer (for barrier and occlusion), and SPF (for finish and comfort).
  • Every step includes 2–3 real product options (affordable, midrange, luxe) chosen for ingredient transparency, proven actives, and top formulation quality.

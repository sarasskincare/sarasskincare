How Our Quiz Works
Every question you answer feeds into a real-time, weighted logic system.
Each response is mapped to specific:
  • Skin profiles (based on dermatology research, which there are more than 50 of that match YOU)
  • Ingredient needs (based on reactivity, pH range, molecular size, solubility, and penetration depth)
  • Routine preferences (like product texture, step count, and budget)
Your answers aren’t just categorized—they’re scored and cross-analyzed. This builds a 3D profile of your:
  • Skin type and barrier strength
  • Active ingredient tolerance
  • Oil vs water balance
  • Repair vs renewal needs
  • Environmental + lifestyle stressors
This gives us the blueprint to start formulation-matching your perfect routine.
How We Build Your Routine
Once your skin profile is complete, we start building your ideal skincare routine—from the ingredients up. Here's how:
  • Ingredient Matching
    Based on your skin goals and sensitivities, we select ingredients with the right:
    • Function (hydration, exfoliation, brightening, calming)
    • Size (to know if it penetrates or stays surface-level)
    • Solubility (to ensure it pairs well with others)
    • pH Compatibility (so it won’t irritate your skin)
  • Step-by-Step Formulation
    We construct your routine in layers, just like a real cosmetic chemist:
    • Cleanser: Based on how much sebum, sweat, or makeup you produce
    • Toner/Essence: Optional — chosen if your skin needs quick hydration or pH balancing
    • Serum: This is the core of your routine — matched to your top goal (e.g., acne, glow, repair)
    • Moisturizer: Chosen based on occlusive vs humectant needs
    • SPF: Always matched to finish, sensitivity, and comfort level
  • Budget + Preference Filter
    Each step gets 2–3 product options, based on your chosen profile:
    • Affordable
    • Midrange
    • Luxe
Every recommendation is formulation-vetted — we read the INCI list, not just the marketing. This quiz doesn’t just give you "dry skin? Use cream."

It mimics how a dermatologist + cosmetic chemist would approach your routine:
  • With a clinical understanding of what your skin needs
  • Using formulas that actually work
  • Built using ingredient layering logic, to avoid conflicts or overload
We use compound-level matching, real user data, and skin biology research to give you a routine that makes sense for your skin.
